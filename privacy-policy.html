Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 28, 2026

SunkenPeak respects your privacy. This policy explains how Follow Through handles your information.

1. Data Collection & Authentication

We prioritize your anonymity. We use Sign in with Apple for authentication. We do not collect or store your name, your personal email address, or your contact details. We only store a unique, anonymized string of characters (User ID) provided by Apple to identify your account.

2. On-Device Video Processing

All video analysis and rally detection are performed locally on your device's hardware. Your videos never leave your device and are never transmitted to SunkenPeak, our servers, or any third party.

3. Server-Side Data (Supabase)

The App uses a secure backend (Supabase) to sync your account status. The only data stored on our servers includes:

  • Your unique anonymized User ID.
  • Your current credit balance (for Free Tier users).
  • Credit usage logs and activity timestamps (for service integrity and analytics).

4. Children's Privacy

The App is safe for users of all ages as it does not collect personal identity data or require the sharing of contact information.

5. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You may request the removal of your data at any time via the "Remove Data Request" button in the App settings. Triggering this will:

  • Permanently delete all credit usage history and app-specific metadata from our servers.
  • Reset your credit balance to zero.
Note: We retain your anonymized User ID on our "exclusion list" solely to prevent the abuse of introductory free credits and to maintain the integrity of our lifetime/subscription license records.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at sunkenpeak@gmail.com.
